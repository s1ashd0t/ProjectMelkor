## 📊 What Makes This Unique

### Synthesis Approach
Instead of single-factor analysis, EcoAlert combines:
- Historical patterns (how has this behaved before?)
- Structural forces (what are the deep drivers?)
- Current events (what's happening now?)
- Contrarian views (what if the consensus is wrong?)

### Time-Horizon Intelligence
Different factors matter at different timeframes:
- **Hour**: Recent events dominate (50% weight)
- **Day**: Near-term momentum key (40% events, 35% patterns)
- **Week**: Tactical dynamics (40% patterns, 30% events)
- **Month**: Structural forces emerge (45% patterns, 30% structural)
- **Decade**: Deep structures dominant (50% structural, 40% patterns)

### Confidence Decay
Predictions become less certain over longer timeframes:
```
Hour:    75% ████████████████████████
Day:     70% ███████████████████████
Week:    62% ███████████████████
Month:   58% █████████████████
Decade:  48% ███████████████
```
